About

BTS is a boy band from South Korea. There are seven members: Kim Namjoon also known as RM is the leader of BTS and their main rapper. The other two rappers are J-Hope and Suga. There are four vocalist: Jeon Jungkook is the lead vocalist and also the youngest of the band. The other vocalist consist of Park Jimin, Kim Seok-jin, and Kim Taehyung. Not only do all members sing/rap but they also dance. J-Hope is their dance leader, followed by Park Jimin and Jeon Jungkook. BTS first debuted in 2013 and have been travelling the world ever since. Things were not easy for them at first but look at them now.
